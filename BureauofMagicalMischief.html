Bureau of Magical Mischief

Participants will fill out an application to be an agent of the Bureau of Magical Mischief. We will review applications and create a handful of custom missions for them which we think will be entertaining and insightful and helpful and kind. When they arrive at the event, they will scan their ID card at the device or input an ID number, which will keep track of the missions they have completed so far, and offer them a choice of a new one. The missions will be designed to do one or more of: build self-confidence, get unstuck, push boundaries, connect with others, discover something about oneself or one's relationships, etc.
