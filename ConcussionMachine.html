Concussion Machine

Description for the Jury

A roller derby demolition track with soapbox derby style pedal cars designed to withstand collisions. Participants can pedal the cars around and smash into each other. If making cars that can turn and a track that is big enough turns out to be too much, we will make a raised track with cars powered by gravity that will smash into each other at the bottom.
