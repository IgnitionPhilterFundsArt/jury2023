Dating Bracelet Booth

Dating Bracelet Booth is coming back to Flipside! The goal of DBB is to assist fellow burners with making love connections, whether it"s long term or for one night. It was designed with the purpose to especially assist non-neurotypicals and shy people, individuals of all sexual orientations and identities in being more confident with approaching others. The way it works is participants make a special bracelet that tells others that they are open to be approached but also what and who they are into. They are provided with a decoder card so they can easily identify those who might be interested in them before even starting a conversation. Love is in the air, but many of us need just a little help finding it.
