Dating Bracelet Booth

Description for the Jury

Dating Bracelet Booth is coming back to Flipside! The goal of DBB is to assist fellow burners with making love connections, whether it"s long term or for one night. It was designed with the purpose to especially assist non-neurotypicals and shy people, individuals of all sexual orientations and identities in being more confident with approaching others. The way it works is participants make a special bracelet that tells others that they are open to be approached but also what and who they are into. They are provided with a decoder card so they can easily identify those who might be interested in them before even starting a conversation. Love is in the air, but many of us need just a little help finding it.

Description for the Website

Dating Bracelet Booth is returning to Flipside to help participants find a love connection! Come by the booth and follow simple instructions to make yourself a beautiful bracelet so that others know you are interested in meeting someone for a romantic and/or sexual and/or kinky connection here at the burn! Then when you see someone wearing DBB bracelet, take that as an invitation to start a conversation. Use your decoder cards to see if you are into the same things. If you have your bracelet from last year's Flipsides or from UBS please plan on bringing your bracelet with you to meet people. And stop by to update your bracelet if anything changed in your preferences since you made it. Love is in the air and DBB is here to help you find it!
