Disco Demolition PineCar Derby

Description for the Jury

We are creating an arena and experience! The arena will consist of 4 ramps that meet in the center. Each ramp is about 3 feet high and 6-8 feet long (the surface is flexible, so exact length will be determined as we build). We hope to make a large arch between each opposite ramp, and drape with silver fabric to evoke a disco theme. A "disco plated" car will hang from the center of this canopy. We are looking at light strips, paint, flag-banners, and other decor to enhance the festive atmosphere. Pyropolites will be invited pre-event to make their own tiny art cars to show off, to race, and to smash into one another. We will have some car-making supplies and hand tools at Flipside for anyone who wants to create a car on the spot. We will have a DMV style check in. We will hold at least 2 formal exhibition/competition events. We may leave the arena open (if not abused) for use when we are not holding an event. We hope to have patches for participants and prizes - categories and prizes TBD.

Description for the Website

Did you ever want to build an art car, but thought, "I don't have the space, bandwidth, cash to do that!"? Well THIS is your opportunity to build a pint-sized, pinewood (-based) car to show off (and maybe smash up!) at our Disco Demo Pinewood Derby! We are bringing the arena for all the action, a pit crew to help direct it, and prizes galore! Parameters for participant vehicles and opportunities to build them coming soon!
