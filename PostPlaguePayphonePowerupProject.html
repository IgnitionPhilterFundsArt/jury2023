Post Plague Payphone Powerup Project

Description for the Jury

F U Telecom is attempting to rebuild in a more lasting fashion after our pandemic hiatus and is in need of some assistance. Much of our equipment was powered by lead acid batteries, and those batteries have a finite lifespan even when unused, not to mention they're extremely heavy! We have a plan to build custom battery packs for our payphone stations using repurposed good condition lithium cells that will fit entirely inside the phone booths, locked away from unwanted participant vandalism and the elements, and that should last a solid decade or better rather than the 3 years the heavy lead batteries got us. For those unfamiliar with FU Telecom's phone booths, these are arted, brightly lit walk-up payphone booths set up generally on the edges of the event such as by the corral, badlands, greeters, etc, and they serve as both interactive art and as emergency coms people can use to contact Safety HQ, or just their friend in They feature a 10ft high pole with bright blue LEDs to make them easy to locate even across the field, and some of them have PA speakers we've been experimenting with that can play announcements/warnings/etc. F U Telecom has deployed these phones in several iterations at many events since 2016 to great effect, and are working hard to rebuild and build upon our experience and infrastructure to continue doing so at future Flipsides and other burn events in the future. With this art grant, we can take a major step forward in doing so! A $1000 grant gives us enough funding to buy the tools and cells to build batteries for a single phone booth which we can do testing with this year. Each booth beyond the first will cost us substantially less and our fundraising may cover more of them, but the tooling to do the first one is a major roadblock hence we need help!
