The River Project

Description for the Jury

This is a performance art piece for Burning Flipside 2023 that takes advantage of a feature of the land the event is held on to intentionally violate the rule of “No Commerce.” Sales will be conducted legally inside the public waterway or just on the bank, and all profits will be donated to art projects for Flipside 2024 (or another event.) To avoid copycats or advance-war-planning, this project will not be publicized much in advance. However, all financials and other documentation will be made public at the end of the event. 1) We'll be selling items folks might forget at home and need at Flipside. So bug repellant, sunscreen, toothbrushes, wet wipes, lighters, spare flatware, stakes sunglasses, etc. No items which require special regulations or permitting (no alcohol, tobacco, food prepared on site, etc) will be sold. 2) At this time I believe we have the capacity and safety planning to run this about 6AM-8PM Saturday and Sunday. If we expand times, likely we. Would add times on Friday afternoon/evening.
